Our Mission & Goals

Mission Statement

At Iba Recycle and Recovery Hub, our mission is to provide comprehensive recycling solutions that benefit our community and the environment. We strive to reduce waste, conserve natural resources, and promote a circular economy by collecting, processing, and repurposing recyclable materials.

Our Goals

Environmental Protection

Minimize landfill waste and reduce the environmental impact of resource extraction.

Community Engagement

Educate and involve the community in sustainable practices.

Innovation

Continuously improve our processes to maximize recycling efficiency.

Economic Benefits

Create value from waste materials and support local economies.
